League Rules (Draft)

This is a placeholder ruleset. We’ll refine it together.

1) Manager conduct

  • Be respectful. No harassment or cheating.
  • Decisions by admins are final in disputes.

2) Teams & contracts

  • Each manager controls one team.
  • Signings happen through the league transfer system.
  • Budgets and sponsor goals will be enforced.

3) Races & results

  • Races are simulated in-game, results are published on the site.
  • Points systems will be listed per division and race category.

4) Transfers (coming soon)

  • Windows: pre-season and mid-season (subject to change).
  • Free agents can be signed immediately if rules allow.

5) National teams (coming soon)

  • National teams are formed from top riders per country.
